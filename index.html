About me

A decade of IT leadership — now building the cloud.

I've spent ten years in enterprise IT at Mutual of Omaha, growing from front-line support to Technical Lead and Scrum Manager for command-center operations — the escalation point, the mentor, the person who turns messy processes into systems that hold up under pressure.

Over the last few years I've channeled that operational instinct into building software end to end — designing and shipping cloud-native, full-stack products on my own: a multi-tenant lab-analytics platform and an AI-powered booking concierge, from database schema to React interface to deployment.

Now I'm formalizing the cloud side of that work and moving deliberately into cloud solution architecture and full-stack engineering — pairing real enterprise IT discipline with hands-on building. I care about the unglamorous parts: the runbooks, the reliability, the things that don't surprise you six months later.
