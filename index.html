QuicLens

Cloud-native lab analytics platform

A multi-tenant research analytics platform designed for neurodegenerative disease and prion research labs. QuicLens helps researchers analyze experimental data, review fluorescence kinetics, classify plate maps, evaluate dose-response results, and maintain audit-ready records in a cleaner, more structured workflow.

This project is intentionally not an ELN or research notebook. It is focused on analysis, interpretation, and decision-ready outputs for labs working with complex experimental data.

PureZen

AI-powered booking concierge

A booking platform concept with a conversational AI concierge that helps guests move from service questions to confirmed appointments. Instead of forcing users through a traditional booking flow immediately, the concierge supports service discovery, scheduling, rescheduling, and cancellation through a more natural experience.

This project explores conversational UX, API design, appointment workflows, and how AI can support a real business process without making the experience feel overbuilt.

About Me
